Introduction

FiTech Habit Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

üéØ Privacy First Approach

TL;DR: FiTech Habit Tracker is a privacy-first app. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. Everything stays on your phone. We don't use analytics, ads, or tracking. Your habits are yours alone.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

FiTech Habit Tracker stores all data locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We collect and store:

  • Habit Information: Habit names, descriptions, categories, schedules, reminder times
  • Completion Records: Dates and times when habits were marked as complete
  • User Preferences: App theme (light/dark mode), notification settings, week start day, optional user name
  • Statistics: Streak counts, completion rates (calculated from your completion data)

‚ùå Information We DO NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect any personal identification information
  • We do NOT require account creation or login
  • We do NOT access your contacts, camera, or other device features beyond notifications
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • We do NOT display advertisements

How We Use Your Information

All data is stored locally on your device and is used solely to:

  1. Display your habits and completion history
  2. Calculate streaks and statistics
  3. Send local reminder notifications at your scheduled times
  4. Maintain your app preferences (theme, settings)

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All data is stored exclusively on your device using React Native's AsyncStorage
  • No Cloud Backup: We do not automatically back up your data to any cloud service
  • Device Security: Your data security depends on your device's security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.)
  • App Uninstall: All data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

üì¨ Notifications

  • Purpose: To send you local reminder notifications for your habits
  • Usage: Notifications are created and delivered locally on your device at the times you specify

Third-Party Services

FiTech Habit Tracker does NOT integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Payment processors

The app operates entirely offline and does not communicate with any external servers.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not contain content specifically directed at children.

Data Retention

  • Data is retained on your device until you manually delete it within the app or uninstall the app
  • You can clear all data at any time through the app's Settings screen ("Clear All Data" option)
  • No data is retained on our servers because we do not operate any servers

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Delete: Delete individual habits or clear all data via Settings
  • Export: Currently, there is no data export feature (all data is local)
  • Control: Full control over what data you create and store

Technical Details (For Transparency)

Data Structure

Data is stored in AsyncStorage with the following keys:

  • @fitech_habits: Array of habit objects
  • @fitech_completions: Array of completion records
  • @fitech_user_prefs: User preferences object

No Network Access

The app does not make any network requests except:

  • Loading the app from Expo servers during development (Expo Go only)
  • Checking for app updates through the Play Store (standard Android behavior)

Open Source Libraries

The app is built using open-source libraries:

  • React Native
  • Expo
  • AsyncStorage
  • Expo Notifications (local only)
